A note on AI in the NHS

Building a model that works on training data is the easy part. Deploying it safely — so that it improves outcomes rather than causing harm through automation bias, fairness failures, or poor calibration — requires multidisciplinary collaboration between clinicians, data scientists, information governance leads, and patients.

The NHS AI Ethics Initiative and Alan Turing Institute’s guidance are good starting points for understanding responsible deployment.
